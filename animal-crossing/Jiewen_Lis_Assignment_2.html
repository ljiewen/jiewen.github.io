Day 1: Skippy Appearance

Today I met Skippy, the bunny that introduced me to Bunny day. He told me about the event and said that he will be back on April 14 to host the annual Bunny Day.

I got around 20 eggs each, except for Earth Egg which spawns 3-5 times each day on one island. I had to fly to a different island to obtain more.

Day 2 - 3: DIY Recipes

Skippy is gone today. I collected all the DIY recipes through balloons and my villagers. After collecting all the DIY recipes and having 30+ eggs of each kind, I started crafting.

I made all the clothes and furnitures that I could possible make for Bunny Day.

Day 4: Tired of Bunny Day
